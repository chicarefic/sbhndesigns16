About Us

S - B - H - N or spelled as SIOBHAN is a scotish name which stands for "GOD IS GRACIOUS". This brand of Polymer clay earings made by an inspiration of a girl who stepped on a polymer clay earings then she searched on the internet what is the process in making such thing.

Polymer clay is an art medium that is known for its versatality, pliability and simplicity to work with. It is an oven bake modeling material composed of polymers, resins, coloring agnets and fillers which is good material in making fashionable earings.

All items are handmae in SBHN DESIGNS specially made with care and attention. Handmade item may have imperfections but in SBHN DESIGNS assure you that it wont affect its function and purpose.
